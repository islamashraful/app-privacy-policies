Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 25, 2026

Accounts None
User Data Stored on device
Analytics Anonymous only
No accounts, no ads, no personal tracking; anonymous analytics and crash reporting only

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what information Pause - Smart Spending ("the app"), published by Ashraful Islam, collects, why, and how it is used. The app is designed to help you wait before buying non-essential items and does not require an account.

1. Information We Collect

  • Waiting-list content you create - item names, optional prices, notes, statuses, dates, and settings you choose to enter. This data is stored locally on your device and is not sent to our servers.
  • Anonymous usage data - general app activity, such as opening the app or using a feature. This is used only to understand how the app is used and improve the experience.
  • Crash and error data - basic diagnostic information may be collected when the app crashes or encounters an error, so we can find and fix bugs.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • Name, email address, or phone number
  • Payment information
  • Location data
  • Contacts, photos, camera, or microphone data
  • Browsing history or cross-app tracking data
  • Advertising data or data used for targeted advertising

3. Third-Party Services

The app uses PostHog for anonymous usage analytics. PostHog collects anonymous event data, such as which features are used and how often. This data is not linked to your name, email address, or personal identity, and it is not used for advertising. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. PostHog's privacy policy: posthog.com/privacy .

The app uses Sentry for crash and error reporting. Sentry helps us identify and fix software problems. Your waiting-list item names, notes, prices, and decision history are not sent to Sentry. Sentry's privacy policy: sentry.io/privacy .

4. Data Storage

Your waiting-list items, notes, prices, decision history, and app settings are stored locally on your device. We do not operate a user account system for this app and do not store your waiting-list data on remote servers.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

You can delete items in the app. Uninstalling the app removes locally stored app data from your device, subject to your device backup settings. Because we do not hold your waiting-list data on our servers, there is nothing further to request deletion of.

6. Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted at this URL with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

8. Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: hello.islamashraful@gmail.com
