This Privacy Policy explains how Pause ("the app"), published by Ashraful Islam, handles information. Pause is designed to help you wait before buying non-essential items. The app is built around local, on-device storage.

1. Information We Collect

Pause does not require an account and does not ask for your name, email address, phone number, payment information, or location.

The information you enter into the app is stored locally on your device. This may include:

  • Item names you add to your waiting list
  • Optional prices and notes you choose to enter
  • Item statuses, such as waiting, ready for review, bought, skipped, or bypassed
  • Dates used by the app, such as when an item was created, when it becomes ready for review, and when a decision was made
  • App settings, including waiting period, currency, language, onboarding state, and notification preference

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • No user accounts or authentication details
  • No analytics events
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No cross-app tracking data
  • No precise or approximate location data
  • No contact list, photos, microphone, or camera data

3. How Information Is Used

The information stored by Pause is used only to provide the app's core features: keeping your waiting list, calculating review dates, showing your decision history, formatting prices, remembering your settings, and optionally scheduling reminders.

Pause does not sell your data, use it for advertising, or use it to track you across apps or websites.

4. Local Storage

Pause stores app data in a local database on your device. Ashraful Islam does not operate a user account system for Pause and does not store your purchase-delay items on remote servers.

Because your app data is local to your device, it may be included in device backups depending on your operating system and backup settings.

5. Notifications

Notifications are optional. If you enable notifications, Pause uses local scheduled reminders to notify you when an item is ready for review. Notification content may include the item name so the reminder is useful to you.

Pause does not register remote push notification tokens with Ashraful Islam and does not send your item list to a notification server.

6. Third-Party Services

Pause uses Expo/EAS Update technology to check for and download app updates. These update checks may contact Expo's servers for technical delivery of app updates. Your item names, notes, prices, and decision history are not sent by Pause as part of this process.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

Your Pause data remains on your device until you delete items, reset relevant device data, or uninstall the app. Uninstalling Pause removes the app's locally stored data from your device, subject to your operating system and backup behavior.

Because Pause does not store your app data on Ashraful Islam's servers, there is no remote account data to request deletion of.

8. Children's Privacy

Pause is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
